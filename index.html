About Us

Symbiosis Martial Arts Club (SMAC) aims to promote the values of martial arts and provide self-defense training to students of our institute. Martial arts provide a concrete path towards a healthy lifestyle and boost self-confidence. Our mission is to build the community by empowering students of SIT to THRIVE, GAIN strength to overcome obstacles, and INSPIRE others through Martial Arts. To provide a safe haven of positive energy and support for our students' mental and physical growth.

To improve physical and mental strength of students.

To encourage teamwork and belongings.

To promote gender equality.
